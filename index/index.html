On behalf of our constituency I would like to thank the Prime Minister and staff of the World Bank. The Bank’s response to the COVID-19 crisis has been admirable. It has saved lives, scaled up impact and improved our chances of getting back on track towards achieving the Sustainable Development Goals. But the road ahead is long and difficult. Our recovery from the crisis will need to address structural inequalities and be green, inclusive and resilient.
